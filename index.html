Am I Right For You?

Let me first explain a few things that I am not. I am not a developer, designer, UX person, system administrator, DBA, or project manager.

I have been all of these things before, but I won't be them for your organization.

If you've worked with freelancers or an agency in the past, they've had almost all of these people and skills. Can you honestly tell me that you are thrilled with the results?

More than likely you were left feeling lost and anchored. Lost in a completely new world of software development, and anchored to a few key agencies or freelancers who keep everything running.

Whatever the goals of your software product are and whoever you choose to have build it, I want to be there to help you never get lost again or feel tethered to anything other than achieving the goals you had set out to begin with.
